Reforma at night

This is one of the most favorite places to go when you are in Mexico city, there is the "Angel de la independencia" that was inaugurated in 1910 by the then president of Mexico, Porfirio Díaz, to commemorate the Centennial of Mexico's Independence.

Chapultepec

In 1845, the forest was used as a practice place for the cadets of the Military College, now it is one of the most beautiful and famous parks in the world in the 20th century

Latino american tower

The Torre Latinoamericana was inaugurated on April 30, 1956. It gained worldwide prestige as one of the buildings that survived earthquakes such as the one in 1957, 1985 and 2017. And although it is not currently the tallest skyscraper in Mexico City, it is one of the most prestigious for its innovative and courageous construction. Today, the Torre Latinoamericana is one of the safest buildings in the entire capital.

La Mexicana

It has 100,000 square meters of forest area, 60,000 square meters of grassland, an open-air theater, courts for different sports activities, an artificial lake, 4.3 kilometers of bike path, 3.4 kilometers of jogging path, dog garden, a skatepark and a restaurant area. with various proposals, among other various attractions. one of the most beautiful parks in the city

Revolucion

The monument to the revolution, completed in 1938. It is currently one of the most symbolic in Mexico City.

Reforma
